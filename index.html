Project Overview - The Charleston (Copyright 1992 Computer Aided Designs, Inc.)

For the Course Business Industries 10 at Sierra College in the Spring 2026, we were tasked with developing AutoCAD Drafting skills. With the guidance of the instructor Norah Foster, we were able to Draft a Project Drawing Set for the Charleston Plan. The Charleston Plan is a 1 story, 3 bedroom, 2 Bathroom, 2 car garage, 1,973 square foot home. The project drawing set includes a site plan, floor plan, roof plan, and 4 elevations.
